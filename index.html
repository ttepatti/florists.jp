ABOUT FLORISTS

FLORISTS was started in 2016 as a movement to introduce some of the lesser known pieces of Japanese car culture to a worldwide audience.

Starting with importing Japanese parts, FLORISTS has since grown into a brand itself, looking for ways to pair worldwide automotive enthusiasts with the unique parts and culture of Japan.

Now, we continue pushing and striving to give you the most complete picture of car culture in Japan. From the importing of authentic parts to the creation of our own Japanese-inspired clothes and parts, we love Japanese cars just as much as our customers and can accommodate customers of any knowledge level or need.
